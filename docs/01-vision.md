# Vision

THABAT is a modern spiritual journey platform that helps individuals build consistency in worship through encouragement, mentorship, and measurable progress rather than guilt or punishment.

The platform is designed to strengthen long-term habits by focusing on consistency instead of perfection.

Rather than acting as a religious checklist, THABAT serves as a companion that motivates users, assists mentors in providing guidance, and offers administrators meaningful insights into community progress.

---

# Mission

Help people remain consistent in worship through positive reinforcement, habit tracking, mentorship, and meaningful progress visualization.

---

# Core Philosophy

The application must never shame the user.

Every screen, message, notification, report, chart, badge, and animation should encourage improvement instead of highlighting failure.

Our goal is to make users return tomorrow, not feel guilty today.

---

# Main Principles

## Consistency over Perfection

A person who maintains small consistent acts is more successful than someone who performs many acts for only a few days.

The system should reward consistency more than quantity.

---

## Positive Reinforcement

Notifications should motivate.

Reports should celebrate progress.

Charts should highlight improvements.

Achievements should recognize effort.

---

## Mentorship

Mentors are guides.

They are not supervisors.

The platform exists to improve communication between mentors and users.

---

## Flexibility

Everything should be configurable.

Administrators should be able to:

- Create Levels
- Edit Levels
- Remove Worship Items
- Add Worship Items
- Create Missions
- Edit Missions
- Configure Scores
- Configure Notifications
- Configure Achievements

without writing code.

---

## Enterprise Ready

The project should be scalable.

Architecture should support:

- thousands of users
- multiple regions
- multiple organizations
- multiple administrators
- multiple mentors

without architectural changes.
